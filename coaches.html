Coaches

Meet our coaching staff — experienced, certified and dedicated.

Riski James

Head Coach

Short bio: Riski James, Founder and Head Coach of Freetown Jazz Academy, is a pioneer in Sierra Leone basketball. He led the academy to host the nation’s first-ever international basketball game on October 12, 2024, and built three competitive teams at the National League—Division 1 Men’s, Division 2 Men’s, and a Female Team. Passionate about empowering youth, especially girls, through sports, Coach Riski is shaping champions on and off the court. Experienced coach focusing on fundamentals and player development.
